WHAT IS ETHWEI

A new foundation for the onchain economy

Ethwei is a Layer 1 blockchain designed from the ground up to give users real control over their financial lives. By combining fast finality, low fees, and a permissionless architecture, Ethwei makes it possible for anyone — anywhere — to hold, transfer, and grow assets without relying on banks or intermediaries.

Built for developers, traders, and everyday users alike, Ethwei is the foundation for a new generation of decentralized applications that put people first.
